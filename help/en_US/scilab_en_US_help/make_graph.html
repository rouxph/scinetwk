make_graph

SciNetwk

Syntax

G=make_graph(name,n,directed,head,tail)

Parameters

name:

string

n:

int (node_number)

directed:

1 or 0 (graph is directed or not)

head,tail:

int matrix of same length m

G:

is a mlist (type is "graph") with 31 data fields

Description

create the graph structure

Examples

tail=[3 7 2 2 1 5 2 6 7 6 4 6];
head=[7 2 3 1 3 1 5 2 6 5 5 4];
G = make_graph('G',1,7,tail,head);
